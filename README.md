# Sales-Customer-Insights-Dashboard-for-Nykaa
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard for Nykaa to analyze sales performance, customer engagement, and brand insights over time. The dashboard enabled stakeholders to make data-driven decisions by tracking KPIs like total orders, revenue, average order value, product category performance, customer location, and traffic source contribution.
Tools & Technologies:
   •	Power BI
   •	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
   •	Microsoft Excel (for data preprocessing)
   •	Power Query

   Key Outcomes:
   •	Visualized key metrics including Total Orders (1000), Total Revenue (1.41M), and Total Products Sold (5629).
   •	Identified Maybelline as the top-selling brand through quantity sold analysis.
   •	Discovered "Makeup" as the highest revenue-generating product category.
   •	Enabled filtering by Purchase Date, Product Category, Brand, Device, and Rating Category for flexible insights.
   •	Tracked customer engagement through metrics like Average Rating (3.0) and Avg Time on Site (15.44 mins).
   •	Analyzed customer locations and revenue by traffic source (Organic, Social Media, Paid Search, Referral).
